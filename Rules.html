Rules of the Sport

Steps

  1. Flip a coin
  2. Winner: Picks a side
  3. Winner: Chooses the one who is serving
  4. Warm up. Rallies (2 mins)
  5. Game starts with a service
  6. Win: The player must score to 11 points unless both players score 10 points. The winner must win with a lead of 2 points.
  7. Sportsmanship: Players should shake hands with each, the referee and opponent's coach.

What Considers as a Point?

The sport's scoring system goes up to 11. Here are the ways a player can score a point:

  • Opponent: Fails the service
  • Opponent: Fails to return a rally
  • Opponent: Hits the ball twice
  • Opponent: Free hand touches the table
  • If the ball bounces twice on the same side of the table.
  • If the ball is hit into the net
